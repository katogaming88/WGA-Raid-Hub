Roster Signups CLOSED
Open signups to show the Sign Up button on the landing page for applicants. Close it to hide the form between signup periods.
Submitted signups appear in the queue below -- Approve moves them to the Pending Roster sub-tab; Deny marks them rejected.
On the Pending Roster sub-tab, click Add to Roster on each card to add them directly to the roster sheet. Remove dismisses the entry without adding them.

Toggling signups shows or hides the Sign Up button for applicants.

Received Item Requests
Raiders submit requests for items received outside of raid (M+, Great Vault, Crafted, Catalyst, Bonus Roll).
Approve marks the item as received in their loot history, which affects loot fairness scores and priority standing.
Reject dismisses the request with no change to their record.
Officers can also mark items received directly from a player's profile card on the Roster tab -- this bypasses the approval queue and takes effect immediately.
BiS Submissions CLOSED
Open submissions to show the BiS submit form to raiders. Close it when you don't want unsolicited updates.
To let a specific player submit outside of an open window, use the per-player Allow BiS Submit toggle in their officer profile card on the Roster tab.
Approve updates the player's BiS link in the Roster sheet. Reject discards the submission without changing anything.

Toggling submissions shows or hides the BiS list submit form for raiders.

Season Start Date
Season reset workflow:
  • Set the new Season Start Date below so raids before it are excluded from attendance scoring.
  • Update the Season Name (e.g. "Season 2") so new loot imports are tagged correctly.
  • Go to Loot > Import History and click "Clear All Loot" to remove last season's imported loot.
  • Re-import this season's loot via Loot > Import.
  • Click Refresh from WCL on the Attendance tab to pull the new season's raids.
